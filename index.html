What We Offer

OUR SERVICES

From panel casting to final road formation — we provide end-to-end for RE wall and road construction projects.

01
🏭
Casting of RE Panels
Our in-house casting yards produce high-quality Reinforced Earth (RE) facing panels. We use precision steel moulds, M40-grade concrete, and certified reinforcement to ensure every panel meets project specifications and IS code requirements.
  • Dedicated casting yard with steel proprietary moulds
  • M40 / M45 grade concrete as per design
  • Rebar cage fabrication and placement
  • Wet curing and quality inspection
  • Stack storage and dispatch management
  • Batch production for large-scale projects
02
🏗️
Erection of RE Panels
Expert installation and erection of RE facing panels using pick-and-carry cranes and trained rigging teams. We ensure precise alignment, correct panel facing, geogrid layer placement, and compaction of engineered fill to achieve structurally sound RE walls.
  • Escorts Hydra & ACE crane operations
  • Panel plumb and level setting
  • Geogrid layer installation at specified intervals
  • Structural fill placement and compaction
  • Coping and top element installation
  • Final alignment checks and sign-off
03
🛣️
Level Pad Construction
We construct precision-levelled concrete pad foundations at the base of RE walls. Level pads are critical to ensure correct panel bearing, plumb walls, and long-term structural performance. Our teams use laser levels and surveying tools for accurate placement.
  • PCC level pad casting in trenches
  • Surveying and level marking
  • Trench excavation and preparation
  • Correct elevation as per design drawings
  • Curing and inspection before panel erection
04
⛏️
Road Formation & Earthwork
Comprehensive road formation services including bulk earthwork, embankment construction, subgrade preparation, and compaction. We work with motorgraders, compactors, and tippers to build stable road formations ready for pavement layers.
  • Bulk earthwork and embankment filling
  • Motorgrader profiling and trimming
  • Compaction to required Proctor density
  • Subgrade levelling and shaping
  • Camber and cross-fall as per design
05
🚚
Material Transport & Logistics
We manage the complete logistics chain — from transporting cast RE panels from yard to site via low-bed trailers, to managing aggregate and fill material supply for construction. Our fleet ensures timely delivery and safe handling of oversized loads.
  • Low-bed trailer transport for RE panels
  • Route survey and permission coordination
  • On-site panel stacking and management
  • Aggregate and soil material haulage
  • Equipment mobilisation and logistics
06
📋
Project Management & QA
Our experienced project managers and site engineers oversee every stage of construction with rigorous quality assurance procedures. We maintain complete documentation, material test records, and compliance reports for client submission.
  • Dedicated site engineers and supervisors
  • QA/QC documentation and records
  • Third-party lab test coordination
  • Progress reporting and photography
  • DPR and as-built drawing submission

Our Story

ABOUT US

Over a decade of building structure that connects communities and drives development.

Mahim Project Solution is a specialized road and structure construction company with over 12 years of experience delivering high-quality RE panel systems and road construction works across South India.

We were founded with a single mission: to build roads that are structurally sound, built on time, and built to last. From our own casting yards producing Reinforced Earth facing panels, to our experienced erection crews with a full fleet of cranes and equipment — we control every stage of the build.

"We don't just build roads — we build the backbone of regions."

Our core specialisation lies in Reinforced Earth (RE) wall systems — from designing and operating casting yards, to precision panel erection and engineered fill placement. We have successfully delivered projects for NHAI, state PWD departments, and major EPC contractors across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, and Karnataka.

Our team of qualified engineers, experienced supervisors, and skilled tradespeople work together to ensure every project is completed safely, on schedule, and to the highest quality standards.
